Kickstarter
Ryan Shue and Lorenzo de Simone are current sophomores at Milton Academy. After working during the summer and organizing a preliminary fundraiser, they started prototyping different designs for their own vertical farm. They came up with a design, but lacked the funding to build it, so they decided to engineer the Canopy instead, a vertical farm designed for the home. After a few months of constant iteration, they released their prototype on Kickstarter and were successfully funded.
